Biotechnology – Career Opportunities, Pros & Cons

Presented by: Laveeza Khan

Date: 11/05/2025

Introduction to Biotechnology

Biotechnology is the application of biology and technology to create useful products. It plays a role in medicine, agriculture, food, and environment.

  • Uses living organisms or cells
  • Examples: vaccines, GM crops, insulin

Career Opportunities

  • Biotechnologist
  • Genetic Engineer
  • Biomedical Engineer
  • Clinical Research Associate
  • Pharmaceutical Scientist
  • Bioinformatics Specialist
  • Agricultural Scientist

Pros of Biotechnology Career

  • High demand and innovation
  • Global job opportunities
  • Meaningful work (health, environment)
  • High earnings potential

Cons of Biotechnology Career

  • Long education path
  • Competitive market
  • Slow research results
  • Ethical concerns

Conclusion

Biotechnology is a powerful and growing field with diverse careers. It offers innovation, challenges, and meaningful contributions to society.
